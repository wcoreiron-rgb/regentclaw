---
name: open-pr
description: Prepare branch, commit summary, and PR-ready change notes.
---

1. Confirm not on `main` and branch is scoped to the task.
2. Confirm diff excludes unrelated files.
3. Summarize:
   - what changed
   - why
   - validation evidence
   - risks or follow-ups
4. Use clear, conventional commit wording.
5. Include rollout or restart notes for backend/frontend changes.
